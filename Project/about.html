Gnome Thornbreaker

At the age of 143, Gnome Thornbreaker had traveled the 4 continents, visited the lands of Man, Orc, Ire, and Manuh, and fought in the Great War of Meh. After so much travel, Thornbreaker has decided to live a quieter life and enjoy his time with his wife and help new adventurerers on their quest for glory.
