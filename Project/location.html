Directions from the East Gate:
  1. Pass through the East Gate and continue straight when the road comes to a crossroads.
  2. You will pass 9 orange buildings and the castle will be directly in front of you.
  3. Another crossroads will be before the castle gate. Turn right.
  4. Following the path, you will pass a fountain. Continue on the path as it makes a hard left.
  5. Continuing on the path, Thornbreaker's Apothecary will be on the right. It looks like a Gnome Hat!
Directions from the West Gate:
  1. Pass through the West Gate and continue straight when the road comes to a crossroads. You should see a market on the left side.
  2. Continuing down the path, there will be 8 orange huts and the castle will be directly in front of you.
  3. Another crossroads will be before the castle gate. Turn left.
  4. Following the path, you will come to a fountain and a split in the road. Turn right.
  5. Continuing on the path, Thornbreaker's Apothecary will be on the left. It looks like a Gnome Hat!
